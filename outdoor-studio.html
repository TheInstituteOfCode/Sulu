MARBLE GARDEN STUDIO IN CEMAGI, BALI

Welcome to the marble garden - a vast, open space 25m x 13m, filled with marble pebbles & textured limewash walls, its minimalist in all the right ways.

You’ll find lush gardens at one end, and a hidden reading nook framed by the branches of a 50 year old tree. It’s got a wide space for building sets and lots of negative space and stunning natural light & shadows.

And at the other end you’ll find a smaller tree and grassy hill, with a stone feature wall behind it and an antique looking ‘shed’ with aged timber doors.

MEDITERRANEAN GARDEN IN CEMAGI, BALI

Welcome to the med terrace, a diverse outdoor area with a variety of different spaces to shoot in.

With a custom-built italian style outdoor kitchen setup, a stunning staircase and private rooftop and lush wildflower gardens this is a studio that transports you straight to the mediteranean.

Start with a blank canvas and make it your own, or let our team style it for you with timber table, linen tablecloths, handmade pottery and fresh vases of flowers.
