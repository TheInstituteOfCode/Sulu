Want to book a test shoot at Suluh?

We love to support the creative industry, and set aside time every month for free collaborative test shoots, pitch us your concept below.

planning a test shoot

You can request anything from a 2-hour block to a full day, and either a single space (indoor or outdoor) or the entire studio. We have limited capacity and can’t guarantee we’ll be able to accommodate every request but encourage you to reach out and let us know what you have in mind.

A few little Ts & Cs to keep in mind -- tests shoots are for non-commercial usage only, which means nobody on set should be being paid to be there and the final images can’t be used commercially to promote any products or services. The purpose of the shoot is for photographers, models, stylists and hair and makeup artists to flex their creativity and add a fun project to their portfolio or to test out the space and capture reference images you can use to pitch a future shoot to a client.

Any content captured in the test shoot, both BTS & the end results should tag @suluhstudio and if we re-share it we’ll be sure to credit you (or the entire team you request) in return.
