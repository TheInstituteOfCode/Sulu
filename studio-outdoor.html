MEDITERRANEAN GARDEN IN CEMAGI, BALI

With over a decade of experience running a creative agency in Bali, we designed Suluh to solve every frustration we had with studios in the past.

From making sure the studio is spotlessly clean when you arrive, to convenient flow for booking check-ins and check-outs to having everything you need for a smooth shoot day (like as many tables as you need, and more than enough clothing racks and coat hangers).

Production delay? We’ve got complimentary rescheduling. Want to setup the day before? Drop-off everything in advance so you can start fresh on the shoot day. Need an extra hand? Book a styling assistant to steam all the garmets or a shoot assistant to help with lighting and gear.
