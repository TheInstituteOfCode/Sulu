INDOOR PHOTOGRAPHY STUDIO IN CEMAGI, BALI

Say hello to Suluh’s professional photography studio, featuring both natural light through 3x huge floor to ceiling windows and fully equipped studio lighting.

Available 7 days per week from 5am till midnight, this is the studio you’ve been waiting for.

Whether you’re shooting a lookbook or e-commerce project, or a lifestyle campaign we’ve got a range of backdrops and set design options to suit every brand and style from bright and white classic cut-out on our cyclorama wall, to soft neutral tones or our rich timber backdrop.

DESIGNED BY PHOTOGRAPHERS, FOR PHOTOGRAPHERS.

With over a decade of experience running a creative agency in Bali, we designed Suluh to solve every frustration we had with studios in the past.

From making sure the studio is spotlessly clean when you arrive, to convenient flow for booking check-ins and check-outs to having everything you need for a smooth shoot day (like as many tables as you need, and more than enough clothing racks and coat hangers).

Production delay? We’ve got complimentary rescheduling. Want to setup the day before? Drop-off everything in advance so you can start fresh on the shoot day. Need an extra hand? Book a styling assistant to steam all the garments or a shoot assistant to help with lighting and gear.
